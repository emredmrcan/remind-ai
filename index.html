🤖 RemindAI

Smart reminders that think ahead and help you prepare — not just remember.

💡 Real-Life Problems We Forget

  • 🧳 You remember your flight date, but forget to pack a charger.
  • 🎁 You mark a birthday, but forget to buy a gift.
  • 🩺 You set a pill reminder, but run out without knowing.
  • 📅 You have a deadline, but don’t plan the steps ahead.

RemindAI solves these problems by being proactive and contextual.

✨ What Makes RemindAI Different

It goes beyond basic alerts. It helps you plan and stay ready:

  • 🧠 Context-aware: Understands your reminder and its needs
  • 📝 Suggestive: Offers checklists, supplies, or subtasks
  • 🔁 Follow-ups: Not just once, but with relevant notifications
  • 📦 Tracks stock: Like counting your pills or supplies

🧪 Example Scenarios

🎉 Friend’s Wedding

  • “I have a wedding on Friday.”
  • RemindAI: “Want a checklist?” → Clothes, Shoes, Gift
  • + 3 days before: Wash your clothes
  • + 1 day before: Iron clothes, clean shoes

💊 Medicine Intake

  • “Remind me to take Vitamin D daily. I have 100 pills.”
  • Tracks your intake, warns before stock runs out
  • Asks if you want to reorder and how many pills are left

✈️ Travel Plans

  • “Flight to Paris in 3 weeks.”
  • Packing list suggestions, travel doc checks, accommodation reminders
