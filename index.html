🤖 RemindAI

Smart reminders that think ahead and help you prepare — not just remember.

💡 What's the Problem?

We often remember the main event, but forget the important prep.

  • "Wedding on Friday" ✅
  • "Clean shoes, iron clothes, buy a gift" ❌

✨ What RemindAI Does

It's not just a reminder. It's a smart assistant.

Example: “I have a wedding on Friday.”
RemindAI: “Want a checklist?” → Clothes, Shoes, Gift
+ Wash reminder (3 days before)
+ Ironing reminder (1 day before)

💊 Medicine Tracking

“Take Vitamin D daily. I have 100 pills.”

  • Daily reminders
  • Tracks remaining pills
  • Warns when stock is low
  • Asks if you'd like to restock

  • 📚 Work: Break tasks into subtasks with timelines
  • 🐾 Pet: Vet appointments, vaccine tracking, flea meds
